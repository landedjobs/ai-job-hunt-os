# Sources and provenance

These skills separate durable methods from time-sensitive market facts.

## Policy

- Current compensation, hiring, funding, and company-health claims must be researched when the skill runs.
- An exact benchmark belongs in a skill only when its source, publication date, sample, population, and limitation are recorded here.
- Vendor surveys and platform data must be labeled as such. They are not universal base rates.
- Landed's own corpus is a selected set of companies and postings. It can provide directional comparables when the sample is disclosed, but it is not a complete picture of the labor market.
- If evidence is missing or incomparable, the skill must say “unknown” instead of filling the gap.

## Durable references

- [Agent Skills specification](https://agentskills.io/specification): file layout and front-matter requirements.
- [OpenAI interview guide](https://openai.com/careers/interview-guide): an official example of a company-specific interview process.
- [Haseeb Qureshi, Ten Rules for Negotiating a Job Offer](https://haseebq.com/my-ten-rules-for-negotiating-a-job-offer/): process guidance, not a compensation benchmark.
- [Patrick McKenzie, Salary Negotiation](https://www.kalzumeus.com/2012/01/23/salary-negotiation/): negotiation framing, not a compensation benchmark.

There are currently no frozen market-wide numeric benchmarks in the skills.
